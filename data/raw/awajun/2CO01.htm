Pablo awentsaik carta agaja corintonmaya aidau awetimu
1
Kumpamkagtamu
1 Witjai Pabloitjai, minak Apajuí wakegau asa Cristo Jesusa etsegtukti tusa etegtua awetiuwai, yatsug Timoteojai pujusan atum iglesia Apajuinu aidautijum yaakat Corinto batsatjum nuna, ashí Jesusa nemajin aidautijum Acaya nugkanum batsatjum nunashkam, 2 Apajuí iina Apajiya nunú, iina Apuji Jesucristujai wait anenjamag shiig agkan pujutan amastinme.
Pablo waitiamuji
3 Emematiagmi Apajuí, iina Apuji Jesucristo Apajiya nu, iina Apají wait anenkagtin, Apajuí ashí ichichmamtan sukagtina nunú, 4 nigki ichichmamtanash sujamui ashí ii waitiamunmash, ashí tikich waitiagtaish ditashkam ichichkagtugtinme, Apajuí iina ichichmamtan amasua dutiksag tujamu asa. 5 Wagki Cristo waittsauwa nunisaik jutiish kuashat waituinaji, nunisaik imá Cristuig kuashat ichichmabaush ajutjamji. 6 Jutii waituinag duka, atumesh ichichmamja pujusjum uwemjatajum tabaunum nuninaji; tuja jutii ichichmabau ajutjamaina dushakam, atumesh ii waituinag junisjumek waitiakjumesh, ichichmamja pujusjum emamkesjum katsunjatajum tabaunum waituinaji. 7 Atuminig ebetja dakamu ajutjamji, wagki dekainaji atumek jutii waituinag nunisjumek waitiakjumesh, jutii ichichmamainag nunisjumek ichichmamaidau asagmin.
8 Yatsug aidauh, jutii Asia nugkanum batsamsaja kuashat utugchatnum waittsabiag nu dekaatajum tusan wakegajai. Jutiik tikima atsanja nagkaemainchaunum jegantú asaja, iwakuap pujuttaji tusa dakamainuk atsugtamsabi, 9 nuniaku chicham dekajuawaja mantamnati tibauwa nunin dekapmabiaji; tujash nu dekamtijamabi iik kajintsá anentaimtumatsuk, Apajuí aents jakaunash inanna nuwi kajintsá anentaimtan; 10 nuniau asajin Apajuí jutii iman ishamainnum pujajinish jiigmaki uwemtijamjabi, nuniau asa yamaishkam dutijamsag agkanmitkagmasagké awai. Dutijamu asamtai nigkiap atakea duwish agkanmitkagmatnaita tusa dakamu ajutjamji; 11 atumesh jutii pachijattsajum Apajuí aujkagtakjuminig. Kuashat jutii pachijattsajum Apajuí segatkagtiagtaik, kuashat Apajuinashkam see tiagtatui anenjauch Apajuí sujabau juinag nuna diisag.
Pablo Corinto wechamu
12 Wagki jutii eme anenmamag duka juuwai: Iina anentaimtajiya nu ujapaji Apajuí dekaskejiya nujaish jutiish wajuk imagnisaish pujusbaiji ju nugka juish, tuja atumjai ijunja pujusmayag duish. Jutiik aents ditá yachajin iwainmamaina nunikchabiaji, Apajuí wait anenkagta nujai iwainmamkabiaji. 13 Wi papiin agatjamjim nuig, tikich wají pachisaik atum aujkugmesh antukchamnukesh agatjachbaijime, nuniau asamtai shiig ausampap dekawagtatua tajime, 14 duka machik dekaugme, jutii aidautik atum eme anentam agtinaitag nunú, atumshakam antsagmek jutii eme anentam agtinaitjume tsawan Apu Jesús minitna dui.
15 Nuna aaja kajintsá anentaimu asan, dekatkauk atumná taan wainkatjime tusan anentaimsabiajai, aaja nuniakun jimajá ijagsatjime tau asan; 16 Macedonia nagkaemakun ijagsau asan, waketkunush awentsanuk ijagsatjime tusan. Nuna nuniakui atum minashkam wi Judea weakuish yainkamnujum tau asan. 17 Wi nuna aaja nuniktajai tiuwaitag nunash ¿anentainkasnuk tiuwaitag? ¿Nuniachkunush wainak aents dutikachtinaitak dutikatjai tinu aina nuniaknuk taja? 18 Tujash Apajuí tibaujinak betek umina nu dekagtamji jutii “dutikatjai tiuwaitku dutikashtajai” tatsug nunak. 19 Wagki wi, Silvano, Timoteo aaja Jesucristo Apajuí Uchijiya nu pachisaja ujakbaijime. “Jesucristuk dutikatjai ti, ajuma dui dutikashtatjai” tujakchauwai, ni tibaujinak imatiksag betek umijakui. 20 Wagki Apajuí ashí anagkagtuamua duka, Cristui ashí uminkauwai. Nuadui Apajuí emematuinakuik: “Aatus atí”, tuinaji Cristo Jesús pachisa. 21 Jutii aidautin, atumnashkam Apajuí etegtamawag Cristuwai ebetjamkauwai; 22 Nuna dutijama niinu etamak selló awajtamkauwai, dutika eemtikak iina anentain Wakanin sujamsajui, ni anagtamamua nu jukitin asajin 23 Tujash wi Corinto minichag nunak, anui taan atumin kakaakan chichagkun wake emeskaijim tusan nunikag nunak Apajuí dekagtawai. Nuna wi dekas tuchataik Apajuí suwimkan sujustí tajai. 24 Jutiik atum dekaskeapi tajum duka iinuk emamaitsuji, jutiik wakegaji atum yaigtasa shiig aneamu ajutjamtinme tusa, dekaskeapi tabauwa nuig ebetmamjajum pujau asagmin.
