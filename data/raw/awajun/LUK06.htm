6
Jesusa jintintaiji aidau tsawan ayamtai aig trigo jukí yuwawagbau
(Mt. 12.1-8; Mr. 2.23-28)
1 Tsawan ayamtai tsawaju ai, Jesús ajak ajakbaunum nagkaebau. Nuniamunum ni jintintaiji aidau trigo nejeen kupik jukiag uwejin pakajag yuwidau. 2 Nuniagtai ujumak fariseo aidau chichajuinak:
—¿Wagka tsawan ayamtai tsawagmataig takantsashti tibauwa nunin aish aikagme?, tuidau.
3 Tiagtai Jesús ayaak:
—¿Atumek agagbauk ausachuk ainajum, David niijai yujamujai yapajuinak, 4 jega Apajuí ememattainum wayá, pag imá sacerdotek yuwati tibauwa nuna yuwak, niina kumpají aidaunash susauwa duka?, tau.
5 Nuna tii, nuigtú chichaak:
—Aentsmaga akiinauwa duka, tsawan ayamtaiya nuna Apujiyai, tiuwai.
Tsawan ayamtai aig Jesús aents jau etsagagbau
(Mt. 12.9-14; Mr. 3.1-6)
6 Ataktú tikich tsawan ayamtai ai, Jesús jega ijuntainum wayau, nunik jintinkagtau; nuniamunum nui aents uwejí untsujiya nu jaka ekeemkau pujau. 7 Nunitai maestro chicham umiktinun jintinkagtin aidau, nuigtú fariseo aidaujai Jesusan dii ijuntuidau, tsawan ayamtai aish etsagagchatpash tusag tsanumjugtasag. 8 Tujash nigka dita anentaibaujinak dekau asa, aents uwejí jaka ekeemkaun chichajak:
—Wajaktá, nunikam ejapen wajantá.
Tama niishkam wajakí ejapen wajantú. 9 Nunikmatai Jesús chichajak:
—Iniastajime: ¿Tsawan ayamtai aina nuwish pegkejan dutikati atsa pegkegchaun dutikati tibaukait; etsagautskek jakati tusa idaimainait?, tau.
10 Nuna tii ashí aents nui ijunun diisá, nuna aentsun chichajak:
—Uwejum dakuekta, tiuwai.
Tama dakueku, nunik uwejenig pegkeg wajasui. 11 Dutikamtai ditak senchi kajekag, Jesusash utugmainkita tusag chichaidau.
Jesús ni apostolji agtin aidau etejamu
(Mt. 10.1-4; Mr. 3.13-19)
12 Nunú tsawantai Jesús Apajuí ausatatus mujanum weu, nunik nui kashi pujus Apajuí au aujkawa tsawampajui. 13 Nunik tsawag jintintaiji aidaun untsukui, dutika nunú aina nuiyan doce etegjauwai, nunú aidaun apóstol tusa adaijauwai. 14 Duka jujú ainawai: Simón, Pedro atatme tibauwa nu, Andrés Simogka yachi, Jacobo, Juan, Felipe, Bartolomé, 15 Mateo, Tomás, Jacobo Alfeo uchijí, nuigtú Simón Zelote tutai aajakua nu. 16 Judas Santiago uchijí, nuigtú Judas Iscariote Jesusan sujukua nu aatus.
Jesús kuashat aents jintintuamu
(Mt. 4.23-25)
17 Nunik mujanmaya ditajai ijunag akaiki, pakanum wajantú, nunikmatai kuashat aents tuwagkaju ashí judeanmaya, jerusalegnumia, Tiro nayantsa uwet awa nuiya, sidognumia aidau, ni jintinkagtamujin antukagtatus, nuigtushkam dita jaamujinash etsaentugti tusag, 18 nunikag wakan pegkegchau egkemtugbau aidaush tsagagkaju. 19 Ashí aents aidauk Jesusan antigtatus kakanmamaidau, niiniya senchi jiinak ashí aents jau aidaunak etsagau asamtai.
Shiig aneasagtin waittsagtinjai tibau
(Mt. 5.1-12)
20 Jesús ni jintintaiji aidaun diisá chichajak: “Shiig aneastajum atum ujunauch aidautigmek, wagki Apajuí inama duka atumdau asamtai”.
21 “Shiig aneastajum yamai yapajautigmek, wagki shiig juwakagtin asajum. Shiig aneastajum atum yamai buututigmek, wagki shiig aneakjum dushigtin asajum.
22 “Shiig aneastin atajum, Aentsmaga akiinau nemaju asagmin aents aidau kajegtamainak, yajá pujusta tusa jiipainak, pegkegchau chichagtamainak, daagmin adaisajash pegkegchau tujamainakuish. 23 Nunú tsawantinig shiig aneakjum dakujustin atajum, wagki atum jukitnuk shiig muun nayaimpinum ajutjamaidau asamtai; wagki aatus dutikajakú ainawai atumi muunjum, profeta aajakajua nunashkam.
24 “¡Tujash wait anentajime atum wiyakuch aidautigminak, wagki ju nugka juwig atum shiig aneamuk ajutjamaidau asamtai!
25 “¡Wait anentajime atum yamai shiig yuwá batsatutigminak! wagki atak yapajuawagtin asagmin.
“¡Wait anentajime atum yamai dushit batsamtautigminak, wagki wake besemag jegagtamkui buuttin asagmin!
26 “¡Wait anentajime atum ashí aents pegkegnum chichajam aidautigminak! wagki atumi muunjum aajakajua dusha wait profeta aidaun imatikas chichajujakú aina dui.
Shiwajui aidau aneamu
(Mt. 5.38-48; 7.1-2, 12)
27 “Tujash atum mina antugtautigmin tajime: Atumi shiwajum aidaush aneetajum, kajegtamaidaushkam pegkegnum yaigtajum; 28 pegkegchau chichagtamaidaush pegkegnum chichagkatajum, wainak tsanumjutpainakuish Apajuí aujtustajum. 29 Yapinum awatamkujaish, tikich yapiminish awapiti idaitusta; capa*“Capa”, tawa duka awantak nugkutai esajam aajakui. Lana nuniachkush cabra uje nuniachkush ujuch najanamu tsetsemaku nuniachkush dasenkui dukumatai. Diistá Mt. 9.20. jujutjamkujaish tunicagmesh†“Túnica”, tawa duka jaanch nugkutai esajam tikishia imaan jegaaku aajakui. Duwap nuniachkush uje nuniachkush lana nuniachkush lino najanamu aajakajui. Ujunauch aidauk imá tunicanak nugkujakajui. sujittsuk susata. 30 Makichik aentskesh segapakuig susata; tuja amina wajiigmin jujutjamkuishkam, awagtugkita tiipa. 31 Atum tikich aidau shiig awajtuktinme tusajum wakejin ainajum nunisjumek tikichish shiig awajkatajum.
32 “Atumin anempa duke atumshakam aneakjumesh, ¿wají imana juwagme? Wagki aents tudau aidaushkam dita aneniamujaig aneninawai. 33 Atumin shiig awajtamaina duke shiig awagmakjumesh ¿wají imana juwagme? Wagki aents tudaugtin aidaush nunak nunin ainawai. 34 Atum kuichkin ekes awagtugmamaina duke tsagkatkugmesh ¿wají imana juwagme? Wagki aents tudaugtin aidaushkam penpeentunisag tsagkatdayin ainawai, uwaegak jukitag tuidau asag. 35 Atumí shiwajum aidaush aneetajum, nuigtush shiig awajkatajum, nuigtushkam tsagkatkatajum, niish minash jutiksag iktukti tusa dakatsuk, aatus nuniamunum wají shiig pegkeg jukitnuk ajutjamtinai, nuniakjum Apajuí ashí tikich aidau nagkaesau muunta nuna uchijí atatjume; wagki nigka pegkejai, niina see tuchau aidau, pegkegchau aina nujaishkam. 36 Wait anenkagtin atajum, atumin Apa wait anenkagtina ibau.
Tikichi pujutji pachisjumek augmatigpa tibau
(Mt. 7.1-6)
37 “Tikichi pujutjig augmatú yujaigpajum, atumesh dutiksagmek tama aijum. Suwimkash sukagtusaipa amesh suwimak suwam aim, tsagkugkagtugtajum dutiksaik tsagkujam aminjum. 38 Tikich atsumau wainkugmek susatajum, atumnash Apajuí amastinme tikich atsubau wajuk suwagme dutiksag. Nigka shiig kuashat atum atsumamu nagkaesau amastatjume.
Tikich augmatigpa tibau
(Mt. 7.3-6, 15-23; 12.34-35)
39 “Jesús junashkam augmatak tau: ¿Wainmachua dusha tikich wainmachunash jintanash inaktumainkaih? ¿Mai waanum iyaumainchaukaih? 40 Jintintaiya duka jintinkagtina nuna nagkaesauchui. Untsu papí augbaujin ashimkatta dui, niina jintinta iman wemainai.
41 “¿Wagka yatsumi jiin chipum tekani egkemaush diyagme, atumi jiimin numi egketa nu diitskesh? 42 ¿Utugsamea amina yatsumish: Yatsuju jasta chipum tekani jiimin egketa nuna jigkitjame tumainaitme? ¡Tsanumin aidauwah! Dekatkauk amina jiimin numi egketa nu jiiktá, nunikam shiig wainkam yatsumi jiin chipum tekani egketa dusha jiigkita.
Numik niina nejeen dekataiyai
43 “Numi pegkejak pegkegchaunak nejemaitsui, numi pegkegchaushkam pegkegnak nejemaitsui. 44 Wagki makí makichik numi aidauk ni nejea dui dekanui; untukjak munchiinak nejetsame, antsag uvashkam zarzanmayag juushtayame. 45 Aents pegkejak pegkegnak chichauwai, wagki pegkeja duka anentain ajamu asa. Nunisag aents pegkegchaushkam pegkegchaunak chichauwai, wagki pegkegchauwa duka anentain ajamu asa; wagki shiig kuashat anentain aja nunak, chichaak iwainawai.
Jegamkatasa ajiagbau pachisa tibau
(Mt. 7.24-29)
46 “¿Wagka: ‘Apuh, Apuh’ tujutjume wi taja nu umiachiatkugmesh? 47 Ashí aents minai tanta, mina chichamjun antugtukag imatiksag umidaunak wajijai betekaita nuna titajai. 48 Duka aents jegá jegamkatatus initak tai, kaya muunnum dekanmitkak ajiamun, namak piyujak jegan senchi tukuakush, kayanum dekanmitkakbau asamtai machikish umushkachua numamtinai. 49 Tujash mina chichamjun antayatak umigtachuk, aents jegá jegamak, ajitsuk nugká awanken ekeni jegamkamun, namak piyujak jegá senchi tukú tegak shiig tsaik ajapauwa numamtinai”.
