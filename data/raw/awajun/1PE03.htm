3
Nuwenau aidau umimain
1 Nunisjumek atumshakam, nuwatkamu aidautigmek atumi aishum aina nuig sumimkatajum, Apajuí chichamega nuna dekaskeapi tachau akushkam, chicham ujachmaitak atum pujamun diisag wakekatnume, 2 dekaskenum Apajuí umigkujum imagnisa pujamun waipainak. 3 Atumek jaanch pegkeg iwajamu aina ibau nugkugjum awantak iwagmamjatasagmek wakemaitsugme. Intashmesh tikich pegkeg diigsatnume tusagmek iwagmaitsugme, nuigtú oro aina imanjai iwagmamjatasagmesh wakemaitsugme. 4 Dekas iwagmamat shiig pegkeja duka anentainum nuigtú wakannumash kakagchauch shiig anentaimkau ata nuuwai, nuna Apajuik wakegas diyawai. 5 Nuwa pegkejam duwik batsamajakajua dushakam, Apajuiyaig kajinas anentaimaidau asag, imá aishinig sumimajakú ainawai. 6 Sarashkam Abrahaman umijak: Mina apug, tujakui. Atumek niina nawanji asajum pegkeja nu takaakjum shiig puyatkas pujumain ainagme.
7 Aishmag aidautigmeshkam nuwemjaig shiig antugdaikajum pujustajum, nuwak kakaakchau asamtai. Apajuishkam nuwanash wait anentau asa pujut nagkanchaun susauwai. Atum nuwemjai shiig pujushtaik, Apajuishkam ni augmash antugtamkachagtatui.
Ashí Cristo nemajuidau nunimainji aidau
8 Inagnamun tajime, ashí atumek shiig anendaisa pujustajum, betek nuninuk anentaimkau, dekaskenum yatsut anendaisajum aneniakjum. Wait anenkagtin, nuigtú imanchauch atajum.
9 Pegkegchau awajtamainakuish, pegkegchaunmag iikaigpajum, pegkegchau chichagtamainakuish pegkegchauk chichagkaigpajum. Duka dutiktsuk pegkeg chichagkatajum, nuna nuninak pegkeg yumigkia nuna jukitnume tusa untsukbau ainagme.
10 Wagki: “Ni pujutan aneas diyak, pegkeg tsawantan dakujus inagkeaktatus wakegauk,
pegkegchau chichatnak idaisati, tsanumsá chichat aina nunashkam.
11 Pegkegchauk tupanjata nuniakum pegkeja nu takastá,
agkan pujuta nu egaktá nuniakum duke wejitá.
12 Wagki Apu Apajuik pegkejan takaina nuna kuitamak,
dita Apajuí augbaunash antujui;
untsu aents pegkegchaun takaina nunak Apu Apajuik diitnash dakitawai”.*Sal. 34.12-16.
13 ¿Yaita dusha pegkegchau etamainush, atum pegkeja duke takagminish? 14 Tujash atum pegkeg takaakjum waitiakjumek shiig aneastajum. Makichkish ishamkaigpajum nuigtush uwakmamkaigpajum; 15 duka nunitsuk, atumi anentaiminish Cristo atumi Apujum anentsajum eme anentajum, nuniakjum tuke umintsau atajum atum ayamjumaktin, kakagchau nuigtú ajantsá diijatkujum, atum dakamu ajutjamaina nuna pachis tujamainakui ashí ujamaina nu, 16 tujash pegkeg anentaiyai nuigtú eme anentsajum dutikatajum, atum Cristo nemaju asajum shiig pujajum nuna pegkegchau chichagtamainak wainak tsanumjutpaina nu datsagtinme. 17 Wagki pegkeg takaaku waitiamu nu imá pegkejai, Apajuish aatus wakegakuig, pegkegchau takaaku waitmaina nu.
Jesucristo iina iwaintugmak waittsamu
18 Wagki Cristushkam tudau aidaunum makichkia jakauwai, pegkejan takauwa nu pegkegchau takau aidaunum, Apajuiyai jujamkita tau asa. Iyashinig jaak waittsauwai tujash aneantui Wakaní Pegkejiya nuna senchijin, 19 nunik aents jakau aidau wakaní tuke achikbaujai betek batsatbaunum wee chichaman etsegkauwai, 20 yaunchuk Noé pujusmatai batsamajakú intimkiaju aina nuna, Apajuí waugtutsuk arcan najantai dakajakua dui, nu nuniamunum ujumak aents, ocho yuminmag uwemjajui. 21 Nunú yumia duka yamimainag nuna iwainawai, nu aaja nuniaku yamai uwemat juinag nu. Yamimamuk iyashnak pegkeg ematsui, ayatak anentaimtajuish pegkeg Apajuí emtinish atajai tabauwa nuuwai; duka Jesucristo nantakiuwa dui uwemnawai, 22 nigka nayaim waka, Apajuí untsujin eketui; niinig sumimtikamui ángel aidau, wakan senchigtin aidaush, agkajunum inamin aina dushakam.
